Maize & Blue Cupboard

What is Maize & Blue Cupboard

Maize & Blue Cupboard is made to ensure that members of the University of Michigan community have equitable access to healthy, nourishing and nutritious food and the ability to prepare it for themselves and others. No matter if they are on a tight budget, or have a physical restriction making it hard to get to the grocery store. Shopping is by appointment only please make an appointment by visiting our sign up page here.

Hours

Location

We are located in the basement of the Betsy Barbour Residence Hall, Please enter through the Maynard entrance. Please contact us if you need elevator access.
Address: 420 S State St, Ann Arbor, MI 48109
Phone: 734-936-2794

What is provided

Food

We have both fresh and shelf stable food including but not limited to produce, meat, dairy, bread, frozen food and shelf stable food (i.e. cans, rice, tuna, ect.)

Kitchen and Cooking

We have dishes, pots, pans, tupperware, silverware, knives, cutting boards and other kitchenware to help you be able to cook your own food.

Personal and Household

We have trash bags, toilet paper, cleaning supplies, hygiene products, school supplies, baby items and more.

Support

We also offer support getting connected to campus support like CAPS, Dean of Students and Finacial Aid. As well as Michigan's food program SNAP.

Some Things to Know Before Your Appointment

  • Appointments are 15 minutes long to accommodate as many shoppers as possible.
  • If there are no appointments available and you need food we have.
  • emergency bags with non-perishable items that you can pick up.
  • Remember to bring your M-card. It is required to shop.
  • Appointments are for only one person.
  • You can schedule up to two weeks in advance.
  • Shopping will Not impact your Financial Aid.
  • No matter your situation or if you think you need ‘Help’ we are here to support you.
